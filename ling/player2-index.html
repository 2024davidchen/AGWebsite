Player 2 Demands - Part of Speech

Player 2 demands determine what part of speech your word must be.

Learning your parts of speech is very important, as they are the fundementals you need to know in order to learn almost anything in Ling

You should remember the definitions of each part off the top of your head if you want to improve in Ling
